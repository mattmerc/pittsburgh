What is MaptimePGH?

maptime.io/pittsburgh

MaptimePGH

  • MaptimeHQ was born out of a CfA event in 2012
  • A time for learning about mapping
  • Hands-on, beginner-focused, and accessible
  • Emphasis on open source, programming, and web-mapping
  • Using Open Street Map (OSM) as a teaching tool, and enabling/encouraging contributions to OSM
